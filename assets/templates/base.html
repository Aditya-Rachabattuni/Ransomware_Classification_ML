Results

We applied DT, RF, NB, LR, and NN classifiers to classify between legitimate and ransomware samples. Both DT and NN classifiers shows reasonable performance compared to RF. However, LR fails to achieve rewarding F-beta score and recall score compared to other methods, though the accuracy score is reasonable compared to DT, RF, and NN classifiers

Feature Selection

Z-score standardization technique was used to convert each of the variables into a similar scale by centering each of the variables at zero with a standard deviation.
